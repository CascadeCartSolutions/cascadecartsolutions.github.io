Naperville Cart Delivery Progress

The City of Naperville is pleased to introduce a new recycling cart program for residents who are currently receiving curbside recycling service from the City. The new program provides residents with recycling carts made of a sturdy commercial-grade plastic with wheels for easy movement and hinged lids to contain items and minimize animal pilferage and blowing trash. The new carts offer a clean and convenient way for residents to dispose of recyclables and increase their recycling capacity. Beginning Tuesday, September 2, 2014, blue wheeled recycling carts with recycling program information will start being delivered to residents. It is expected to take a few weeks to complete delivery to all residents and once you receive your cart, you should begin using it. The City will no longer pick up recycling from any other recycling container once residents receive their new recycling carts. Residents may recycle their old bins at the Recycling Drop-Off Center at 180 Fort Hill Drive anytime from 8 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Tuesday, Thursday and Saturdays. For more information, visit http://www.naperville.il.us/curbsiderecycling.aspx

Republic Services is the City's recycling contractor. If you have any additional questions regarding recyclable materials, please call Republic Services Customer Service at (630) 723-2800.
